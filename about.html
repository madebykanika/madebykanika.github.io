My Journey to UX ✨

My design career began as a HAPPY accident 💃

Ever since I can remember, I've always loved art and creating new things. Of course, now I know that great design is more than aesthetics, but I think my eye for visuals has definitely helped in my UX career.

It wasn’t until I attended a design thinking workshop at my previous company that it clicked in my mind that my passion in design could be transformed into a full time career. I immediately fell in love with the science & methodology behind design. The exciting problem solving and multidisciplinary nature of UX design inspired me to pivot my career.

After exploring more, I realized that my research background and a natural inclination towards aesthetics will hugely help me design empathetic, meaningful and visually appealing solutions. This realization led me to pursue my master’s degree from Arizona State University with a focus on Human Centered Design.

Ever since then, I am always learning new skills (The newest skill being interaction design).
